Weekly 4: Spaceship Earth?

What was the cultural impact of cybernetics?

Positive Impact: Rise of hippie communes, political egalitarian movements, the birth of the internet
Negative Impact: Justification for continuation of existing oppressive systems. Segregationist movements, etc.

Do you think humans can be understood through cybernetics?

No cybernetics do not allow for change, which is natural for humanity.

Is cybernetics a useful tool for describing reality?

No, it is only useful for planning really, if that. Reality may very well be the opposite of cybernetics . There is no equilibrium or “default setting” in the real world, are we really returning towards anything?
